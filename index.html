 Play It

A minimalistic android music player. (Now unpublished)

A basic music player for the android platform donned with a minimalistic UI. Gestures on the now playing screen includes sliding the image left to skip the songs and also shaking your device to shuffle your songs. With integrated voice recognition simple commands are just a tap away. Basic commands available such as – ‘Play’, ’Pause’, ’Next’, ’Previous’, ’Shuffle’, ’Mix It Up’, ’I’m Feeling Lucky’ and many more. As of May 2016 the app had over 22,000 downloads

  • Date: December 2013
  • Category: Android Application

 Roboduino

A bluetooth controlled car built on android.

A bluetooth controlled car built on an arduino uno. It utilises a 4-wheel differential for turning and navigation. It utilises a bluetoothe module to communicate with an android device via an app that I developed specifically for communicating with the car. My very first arduino project, which utilises a small power bank as the primary battery source.

  • Date: June 2015
  • Category: Arduino Project
